Chris Trottier (Fediverse)

by Charles Iliya Krempeaux

Chris Trottier, who goes by the nickname @atomicpoet, is a notable person on the Fediverse because —

  • he is an influencer on the Fediverse with a large following who talks about the Fediverse and social-media (beyond the Fediverse) in general,
  • #SpaceHost, which is a fully-managed hosting provider for all the Fediverse, that he created with Charles Iliya Krempeaux and Massoud Seifi,
  • GreatApe, which is Fediverse software focused on audio- and video- conversation, which he created with Charles Iliya Krempeaux and Massoud Seifi,
  • Firefish, which is Fediverse software focused on micro-blogging, which Chris Trottier worked on with Kainoa Kanter, and for which Chris does advocacy and marketing.

Work History

As of 2023, Chris Trottier has almost two decades professional experience — entrepreneur, advisor, executive (CMO), business person, marketer, customer support specialist, QA specialist, manager.

Chris Trottier is CMO at #SpaceHost. Was head of QA at Railtown Ventures. Was CMO at the Boldkick marketing agency. Was CMO at the social-media network Manyone. Was head of customer support at Hootsuite, and later moved into marketing. Chris was also a customer support specialist at Microsoft.

Chris Trottier is veteran of the social-media industry. With Charles Iliya Krempeaux and others, Chris previously created what was at one time the most popular Twitter app — i.e., Hootsuite. The app (Hootsuite) was later extended (mostly by Charles Iliya Krempeaux) to support Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram, and other social-media networks. Chris has experience with both the consumer-side, and the enterprise-side of social-media.
